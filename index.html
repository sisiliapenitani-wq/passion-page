Brooklyn Nine-Nine

Welcome to my page about Brooklyn Nine-Nine!

Brooklyn Nine-Nine is a police sitcom and a workplace comedy. It follows the humorous and heartwarming day to day lives of a talented but immature detective and his squad. I have chosen this topic because Broolyn Nine-Nine is a show that I enjoy watching if I ever get bored and it never fails to make me laugh.

Did You Know?

Terry Crews once had to work as a local news sketch artist in real life after the regular artist called in sick. The show used this exact true story for his character in an early episode.
